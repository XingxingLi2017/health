Reservation Notice
Report: you can get your report after 5-7 business days.

Report can be picked up after you get the call from the organization or you can submit a mail request when you complete you checkup

Before examination:

1. Before the physical examination, please have a light diet, do not drink alcohol, and do not work hard. Please fast before the day of the physical examination.

2. It is not advisable to do gynecological and urine examinations during menstrual period.

During examination:

1. The examination items that need fast are blood draw, abdominal B-ultrasound, digital gastrointestinal, gastroscopy and other marked physical examination items.

2. Before the radiological examination, please remove the gold, silver, jade and other accessories you wear.

3. For magnetic resonance imaging, it is forbidden to wear jewelry, watches, mobile phones and other metal objects, and magnetic cards should not be brought into the room to prevent demagnetization.

After examination:

1. After all the items are completed, please be sure to hand in the physical examination form to the front desk.

2. Please keep the results of the physical examination carefully so that they can be compared with the results of the next physical examination, and can also be used as your data when you seek medical treatment.
